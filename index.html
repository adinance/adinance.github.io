EXPERIENCE-TECH CREATOR

CMO GROUP has been founded in Thailand since 1986 and listed on Thailand Stock Market in 2004 with registered capital of 222.5 million Baht, making it the first event company to be registered in the Market of Alternative Investment in Thailand.

CMO Group is a leading business group in building experiences with creativity of ASEAN Region, with integrated services, ranging from event management and marketing communication, entertainment, lifestyle, digital event and hybrid event services covering installation to rental of equipment for image, lighting and sound systems, including the production of video, visual creator and interactive media, with the professional experiences of over 30 years from 8 subsidiaries. This is why CMO Group is acclaimed as one of the leaders of experiential marketing and event management with the most outstanding and creative performances at national and international levels.
