WHO WE ARE

CMO GROUP is a public listed company established in 1986. The company explores all aspect of creative experience covering event management , marketing communications, entertainment services, lifestyle and digital event. As well as equipment rental, production services such as Audio, Visual & Lighting system, video, multimedia and interactive media.

Recently, The company has also repositioned themselves as an ‘EXPERIENCE-TECH CREATOR’. By acquiring tech partners that can accelerate CMO’s tech capability. CMO aims to become a total solution for their clients, providing an end-to-end experience through their creativity and technology.

Our experience business pillars are:

● Events ● Equipments ● Entertainment ● Technology ● Museum Creator
