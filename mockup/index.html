CMO Group has been founded in Thailand since 1986 and CMO Group is a leading business group in building experiences with creativity of ASEAN Region, with integrated services, ranging from event management and marketing communication, entertainment and lifestyle services covering installation  to rental of equipment for image, lighting and sound systems, including the production of video, multi-media and interactive media, with the professional experiences of over 36 years .
Materialising 10,000+ events in the past four decades, CMO Group has been trusted by hundreds local and international firms in delivering the best-in-class experiences in various forms..
Late 2022, our public listed company has repositioned as an ‘EXPERIENCE-TECH CREATOR’ by boosting CMO’s tech capabilities, ushering in the web 3.0 era for our clients and reimagining the potential of the experience industry.
Our 5 experience business pillars are:
Events  -  Equipments – Entertainment - End-to-End 2.0 - Experience 3.0
